# Domain: Competitive Landscape

Last updated: 2026-05-07

## Current Understanding

- **Watch buckle market** is niche — most watch owners use the buckle that comes with their strap
- **Upgrade market** exists among watch enthusiasts who want better clasp/buckle quality
- Competitors range from luxury OEM buckles (Omega, Rolex) to aftermarket options
- Key differentiator: micro-adjust mechanism — not widely available in aftermarket buckles
- Price point ($40-50) sits between cheap Amazon alternatives and luxury OEM ($100+)

### Known Competitors / Alternatives

| Competitor | Type | Price Range | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------------|-------|
| OEM buckles (Omega, etc.) | Luxury brand | $50-200+ | Brand-specific, not universal |
| Generic deployant clasps | Amazon/eBay | $10-25 | Low quality, no micro-adjust |
| Hirsch buckles | Aftermarket brand | $20-40 | Established strap brand |
| Watch strap brands (Delugs, etc.) | Partners | Varies | May be partner, not competitor |
| DIY/custom machined | Hobbyist | Varies | Small scale, niche forums |
