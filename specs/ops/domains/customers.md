# Domain: Customers

Last updated: 2026-05-07

## Current Understanding

- **Primary customer**: Watch enthusiast who owns multiple watches and cares about strap comfort/fit
- Likely male-skewed demographic (watch hobby demographics)
- Probably owns watches in the $200-5000 range (affordable luxury to mid-range)
- Motivated by comfort — the micro-adjust solves the "between holes" problem
- Discovery likely through watch communities, Google search, or ads
- International customer base (watches are global hobby)

### Hypothesized Segments

| Segment | Profile | Buying Behavior |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Enthusiast collector | 5+ watches, active in forums | Buys multiple buckles for different straps |
| Quality upgrader | 1-2 nice watches, wants best accessories | Single purchase, may not return |
| Gift buyer | Buying for watch-lover partner/friend | Seasonal, one-time |
| Brand partner customer | Buys through Junghans/Delugs | May not know Fitwell brand directly |
