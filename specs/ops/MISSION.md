# Mission

Last updated: 2026-05-07

## North Star

Build internal analytics infrastructure that gives Fitwell Buckle Co. full visibility into the customer journey — from first ad impression through purchase to repeat buy — enabling data-driven decisions about where to invest for growth.

## Why This Exists

Shopify's built-in analytics are limited:
- No cross-channel attribution (Google Ads spend vs. organic vs. direct)
- No funnel visibility (who visited the site but didn't buy?)
- No cohort analysis (do Google Ads customers come back?)
- No keyword-level ROI (which search terms drive actual revenue?)
- Limited customer segmentation (buckle size, watch brand affinity)

We need our own data layer to answer the questions that matter.

## Goals

1. **Own the data pipeline** — Shopify, GA4, Google Ads, GSC, PostHog data in one place
2. **Attribution clarity** — know which channels drive revenue, not just traffic
3. **Customer intelligence** — understand who buys, what they buy, and whether they return
4. **Content leverage** — build SEO-driven landing pages that educate and convert
5. **Ad spend confidence** — prove ROAS by channel before increasing budget

## What We're NOT Building

- A customer-facing portal or account system
- A replacement for Shopify checkout
- A general-purpose analytics platform
- A mobile app

## Success Looks Like

- Every dollar of ad spend can be traced to a channel, campaign, and (where possible) keyword
- We know repeat purchase rate and LTV by acquisition channel
- Landing pages rank for high-intent watch buckle search terms
- Campaign performance is visible in a single dashboard, not across 5 tools
- Decisions about ad budget, new products, and partnerships are backed by data
